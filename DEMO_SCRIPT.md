# Skyline Realty — Live Demo Script

**Duration:** 12-15 minutes
**Audience:** Solo real estate agents and small brokerages (2-5 agents)
**Goal:** Sell AI consulting implementation ($2,500-4,000 setup + $500-1,000/month retainer)

---

## Before the Demo (Setup Checklist)

- [ ] Open [skyline-reality.vercel.app](https://skyline-reality.vercel.app) in Chrome (incognito recommended)
- [ ] Open dashboard in a second tab: [skyline-reality.vercel.app/dashboard](https://skyline-reality.vercel.app/dashboard) (password: see .env)
- [ ] Have your email client open to show agent notification emails
- [ ] Phone nearby if demonstrating voice AI
- [ ] Close all other tabs and silence notifications
- [ ] Test the chatbot with a quick message to confirm it's responsive

## Opening Hook (2 minutes)

> "Let me ask you something. When a lead fills out a form on your website at 9pm on a Saturday — how long does it take for them to get a response?"

**[PAUSE — let them answer]**

> "Here's what the data shows: 78% of buyers go with the first agent who responds. Not the best agent, not the cheapest — the *first one*. If you're responding the next morning, you've already lost most of those leads to someone faster."

> "The average agent response time is 2-4 hours. And 40% of leads never get a response at all."

**[Let that sink in]**

> "What if every single lead got a personalized response in under 60 seconds? Not a generic autoresponder — a real conversation about what they're looking for, suggesting actual properties, and booking showings. All while you sleep."

> "Let me show you exactly what that looks like."

## Section 1: The Homebuyer Experience (3 minutes)

**[Navigate to the landing page]**

> "This is a professional real estate website. Your brand, your listings, your neighborhoods. Clean, modern design that looks like it belongs to a top brokerage."

**[Scroll briefly through the page — point out properties, testimonials, stats]**

> "Notice the social proof — response times, homes sold, 24/7 availability. This builds trust before a visitor even talks to anyone."

**[Click the pulsing chat button in the bottom-right corner]**

> "See that gold button? Every visitor sees it. Now watch this — I'm going to pretend I'm a potential buyer."

**[Type: "Hi, I'm looking for a 3 bedroom home in Ballard under 900K"]**

**[PAUSE while AI responds — let the client see the streaming response]**

> "Notice how it immediately understands the neighborhood, the budget, the bedroom count — and starts suggesting specific properties from your inventory. This isn't a form. It's a conversation."

**[Continue chatting — ask about a property's features or neighborhood]**

> "It knows the details of every listing. Square footage, features, neighborhood highlights, commute times. All the questions buyers ask at 10pm that you can't answer until morning."

**[Share contact info: "My email is sarah@example.com and my number is 206-555-1234"]**

> "Now I've given my contact info naturally, in conversation. No forms, no friction."

**[If the AI suggests booking a showing, click to show the calendar embed]**

> "And look — it even offers to book a showing, right here, right now. Cal.com integration, synced with your real calendar."

**[KEY MOMENT — pause and make eye contact with the client]**

> "That entire interaction took about 60 seconds. Your lead went from curious to qualified to booked — without you lifting a finger."

## Section 2: Instant Lead Capture (2 minutes)

**[Switch to the dashboard tab]**

> "Now let's look at what happened behind the scenes."

**[Point to the new lead in the table]**

> "While I was chatting, this lead appeared automatically. Name, email, phone, budget range, timeline, preferred neighborhoods — everything extracted from the conversation."

**[Point to the urgency badge]**

> "See this urgency badge? The AI automatically flags leads as hot, warm, or cold based on their timeline and budget. You know instantly who to call first."

**[Click to expand the transcript]**

> "And here's the full conversation transcript. You can read exactly what they asked, what they're interested in, and what properties caught their eye — before you ever pick up the phone."

**[Show the status dropdown]**

> "As you work the lead, update the status: new, contacted, showing booked, closed. Everything tracked in one place."

## Section 3: Automated Follow-Up (2 minutes)
